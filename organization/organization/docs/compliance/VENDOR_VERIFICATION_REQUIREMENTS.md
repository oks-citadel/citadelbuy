## Africa (AFCFTA)

### General Requirements (All African Countries)

1. **Business Registration**
   - Certificate of Incorporation from local registry
   - Business registration number
   - Tax Identification Number (TIN)
   - Proof of business address

2. **AFCFTA Compliance**
   - AFCFTA Certificate of Origin (for cross-border trade)
   - Compliance with regional bloc requirements (ECOWAS, EAC, SADC, COMESA)

### Nigeria

**Required Documents:**
- CAC (Corporate Affairs Commission) Certificate of Incorporation
- FIRS (Federal Inland Revenue Service) Tax Clearance
- NAFDAC Registration (for food, drugs, cosmetics)
- SON Certification (for products requiring standardization)
- Bank Verification Number (BVN) for beneficial owners

**Regulatory Bodies:**
- Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC)
- Federal Inland Revenue Service (FIRS)
- National Agency for Food and Drug Administration (NAFDAC)
- Standards Organization of Nigeria (SON)
- National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA) - for NDPR compliance

**Data Protection:**
- Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) compliance required
- Data Processing Agreement with platform
- Privacy Policy published

### South Africa

**Required Documents:**
- CIPC (Companies and Intellectual Property Commission) Registration
- SARS (South African Revenue Service) Tax Clearance
- BEE Certificate (Broad-Based Black Economic Empowerment) - Optional but recommended
- POPIA Compliance Certificate

**Regulatory Bodies:**
- Companies and Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC)
- South African Revenue Service (SARS)
- BBBEE Commission
- Information Regulator (POPIA compliance)

**Special Requirements:**
- POPIA (Protection of Personal Information Act) compliance mandatory
- Cross-border data transfer agreements if serving non-SA customers

### Kenya

**Required Documents:**
- Certificate of Incorporation (Registrar of Companies)
- KRA PIN Certificate (Kenya Revenue Authority)
- EAC Certificate of Origin (for East African Community trade)
- Data Protection Compliance Certificate

**Regulatory Bodies:**
- Registrar of Companies
- Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA)
- East African Community (EAC)
- Office of the Data Protection Commissioner

**Special Requirements:**
- Kenya Data Protection Act compliance
- EAC trade certification for regional sales
- COMESA certificate for wider African trade

### Ghana

**Required Documents:**
- Certificate of Incorporation
- TIN (Tax Identification Number)
- Ghana Standards Authority Certificate (for regulated products)

**Egypt, Morocco, Ethiopia**
- Country-specific business registration
- Tax registration certificate
- Import/export license (if applicable)

## North America

### United States

**Federal Requirements:**
- EIN (Employer Identification Number) from IRS
- Business License (varies by state)
- W-9 Form
- DUNS Number (for federal contracts)
- SAM.gov registration (for government procurement)

**State Requirements:**
- Certificate of Authority (state-specific)
- Sales Tax Permit
- State Business License

**Compliance Certifications:**
- **SOC 2 Type II** (Recommended for high-value vendors)
  - Security, Availability, Processing Integrity, Confidentiality, Privacy
  - Annual audit required
  - Demonstrates enterprise-grade security controls

- **CCPA/CPRA Compliance** (California)
  - Required if annual revenue > $25M OR
  - Processes 100,000+ CA consumers OR
  - 50%+ revenue from data sales
  - Privacy policy with CCPA disclosures
  - "Do Not Sell" mechanism implemented

- **PCI DSS** (if handling payment data)
  - Level determined by transaction volume
  - Annual self-assessment or external audit

**Industry-Specific:**
- HIPAA (healthcare products)
- FCC compliance (electronics)
- FDA registration (food, medical devices)

### Canada

**Required Documents:**
- Business Number (BN) from CRA
- GST/HST Registration
- Provincial Business License
- Certificate of Incorporation

**Compliance:**
- PIPEDA (Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act)
- Provincial privacy laws (Quebec: Law 25, BC: PIPA)

## Europe

### European Union (General)

**Required Documents:**
- VAT Number (verified via VIES)
- Company Registration Number
- EORI Number (for customs)
- Certificate of Incorporation

**Mandatory Compliance:**

1. **GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)**
   - Data Protection Officer (DPO) appointment (if processing large-scale data)
   - Privacy Policy compliant with GDPR
   - Cookie consent mechanism
   - Data Subject Rights implementation:
     - Right to Access
     - Right to Erasure
     - Right to Portability
     - Right to Rectification
     - Right to Object
   - Data Processing Agreement (DPA) with platform
   - Records of Processing Activities (ROPA)
   - Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) for high-risk processing
   - Breach notification within 72 hours

2. **VAT Compliance**
   - VIES VAT number validation
   - Intra-community supply documentation
   - OSS (One-Stop Shop) for cross-border sales

3. **Product Compliance**
   - CE Marking (for applicable products)
   - EU Declaration of Conformity
   - Technical documentation
   - RoHS Directive compliance
   - REACH Regulation (chemicals)

**Digital Services Act (DSA):**
- Notice and action mechanism
- Transparency reporting
- Complaint handling system
- (Very Large Platforms: Additional risk assessment and audit requirements)

### United Kingdom (Post-Brexit)

**Required Documents:**
- Company Number (Companies House)
- VAT Registration (HMRC)
- EORI Number (UK-specific)

**Compliance:**
- UK GDPR (similar to EU GDPR)
- UKCA Marking (replacing CE for UK market)
- Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) registration

## Middle East (GCC)

### United Arab Emirates

**Required Documents:**
- Trade License from DED (Department of Economic Development)
- Chamber of Commerce Certificate
- Tax Registration Number (FTA - Federal Tax Authority)
- EMIR Certification (for regulated products)

**Compliance:**
- UAE Data Protection Law (Federal Decree-Law No. 45/2021)
- VAT compliance (5%)
- Economic Substance Regulations

### Saudi Arabia

**Required Documents:**
- CR Number (Commercial Registration)
- Zakat Certificate (GAZT - Zakat, Tax and Customs Authority)
- SASO Certificate (Saudi Standards, Metrology and Quality Organization)
- SABER Registration (for product conformity)

**Special Requirements:**
- Saudization compliance (Nitaqat program)
- G-Mark for SASO certification
- IBAN for Saudi transactions

### GCC-Wide

**Benefits of GCC Certification:**
- Free movement of goods within GCC
- Common external tariff (5%)
- Exemption from import duties for GCC-origin goods

**Halal Certification:**
- Required for: Food, beverages, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals
- Recognized certifying bodies:
  - EIAC (UAE)
  - SASO (Saudi Arabia)
  - JAKIM (Malaysia - internationally recognized)
  - MUI (Indonesia)

## Verification Tiers

### Bronze Tier (Basic)
**Requirements:**
- Business registration verified
- Valid tax ID
- Owner identity verified

**Benefits:**
- Can sell on platform
- Basic seller profile
- Standard commission rates

### Silver Tier (Standard)
**Requirements:**
- All Bronze requirements +
- Bank account verified
- 50+ successful orders
- 4.0+ average rating
- 6+ months account age

**Benefits:**
- Reduced commission by 2%
- Priority customer support
- "Verified Seller" badge

### Gold Tier (Enhanced)
**Requirements:**
- All Silver requirements +
- Financial statements verified
- Regional compliance verified
- 500+ successful orders
- 4.5+ average rating
- 12+ months account age

**Benefits:**
- Reduced commission by 5%
- Featured placement opportunities
- "Top Seller" badge
- Dedicated account manager

### Platinum Tier (Enterprise)
**Requirements:**
- All Gold requirements +
- SOC 2 Type II OR ISO 27001 certified
- Multi-region verified
- 1000+ successful orders
- 4.8+ average rating
- 24+ months account age

**Benefits:**
- Custom commission rates
- Premium placement
- "Enterprise Verified" badge
- Dedicated support team
- API access
- Custom integrations

## Document Submission

### Accepted Document Formats
- PDF (preferred)
- JPEG/PNG (for scanned documents)
- Maximum file size: 10MB per document

### Document Requirements
- Must be in color (for official documents)
- All text must be clearly legible
- No watermarks or alterations
- Documents must be current (not expired)
- Scanned copies must show full document

### Verification Timeline
- **Initial Review:** 1-3 business days
- **Additional Information Requested:** 2-5 business days
- **Final Approval:** 5-10 business days

### Common Rejection Reasons
1. Expired documents
2. Illegible scans
3. Missing required information
4. Name mismatch between documents
5. Unverified business registration
6. Incomplete beneficial ownership information

## Ongoing Compliance

### Annual Renewals Required
- Business registration (if applicable)
- Tax clearance certificates
- Certifications (SOC 2, ISO, Halal, etc.)
- Data protection compliance attestation

### Continuous Monitoring
- Sanctions screening (quarterly)
- Compliance status review (quarterly)
- Performance metrics tracking
- Customer feedback monitoring

### Compliance Suspension Triggers
- Expired critical documents
- Failed sanctions screening
- Regulatory violations
- Sustained poor performance (< 3.0 rating)
- Customer complaints threshold exceeded
