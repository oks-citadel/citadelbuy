# Cross-Border Trade Compliance Guide

## Overview
This guide outlines compliance requirements for international trade on CitadelBuy's global B2B marketplace, covering export controls, import restrictions, customs regulations, and trade agreements.

## Export Controls

### United States

#### EAR (Export Administration Regulations)
**Administered by:** Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS)

**Controlled Items:**
- Dual-use goods (civilian + military applications)
- Commerce Control List (CCL) items
- ECCN classification required

**License Requirements:**
- Destination (certain countries restricted)
- End-user screening
- End-use verification

#### ITAR (International Traffic in Arms Regulations)
**Administered by:** State Department - DDTC

**Controlled Items:**
- Defense articles
- Military equipment
- Related technical data

**Platform Policy:**
- ITAR items PROHIBITED on CitadelBuy
- Sellers must certify no ITAR goods

### European Union

#### EU Dual-Use Regulation
**Controlled Items:**
- Items in EU Dual-Use List (Annex I)
- Nuclear, chemical, biological materials
- Advanced technology

**Requirements:**
- Export license for non-EU destinations
- Catch-all provisions for WMD concerns

### Export Control Regimes
- Wassenaar Arrangement
- Nuclear Suppliers Group (NSG)
- Australia Group
- Missile Technology Control Regime (MTCR)

**Harmonized System:**
- 6-digit international code
- 8-10 digits for national classification
- Required for all cross-border shipments

**AI-Powered Classification:**
- Auto-suggest HS codes based on product description
- Confidence score provided

## Trade Agreements

### AFCFTA (African Continental Free Trade Area)
**Members:** 54 African countries

**Benefits:**
- 90% of tariff lines duty-free
- Preferential market access
- Simplified customs procedures

**Requirements:**
- AFCFTA Certificate of Origin
- 30-45% value addition in member state
- Compliance with rules of origin

### USMCA (US-Mexico-Canada)
**Members:** United States, Mexico, Canada

**Benefits:**
- Duty-free access for qualifying goods
- Streamlined customs procedures

**Requirements:**
- USMCA Certificate of Origin
- Strict rules of origin (especially automotive)
- Labor value content requirements

### EU Free Trade Agreements
**Partners:** 70+ countries including Japan, South Korea, Vietnam

**Requirements:**
- EUR.1 Movement Certificate
- Supplier declaration
- REX number (Registered Exporter System)

### GCC Customs Union
**Members:** UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain, Oman

**Benefits:**
- Free movement within GCC
- Common external tariff (5%)

**Requirements:**
- GCC Certificate of Origin
- Minimum 40% value addition in GCC

## Customs Documentation

### Required for All Shipments

1. **Commercial Invoice**
   - Seller/buyer details
   - Product description
   - HS code
   - Unit price and total value
   - Incoterms
   - Country of origin

2. **Packing List**
   - Detailed item breakdown
   - Weight and dimensions
   - Package numbering

3. **Bill of Lading / Air Waybill**
   - Carrier document
   - Proof of shipment
   - Transfer of title

4. **Certificate of Origin**
   - Attests to country of manufacture
   - Required for preferential tariff treatment
   - Issued by Chamber of Commerce or authorized body

### Additional Documentation (as applicable)

- Export License (for controlled goods)
- Import Permit (destination country)
- Phytosanitary Certificate (plants, agricultural products)
- Health Certificate (food, animals)
- Certificate of Conformity (technical standards)
- Insurance Certificate
- End-User Certificate (controlled goods)

## Incoterms 2020

### Platform-Supported Terms

**EXW (Ex Works):** Buyer arranges everything
**FCA (Free Carrier):** Seller delivers to carrier
**DAP (Delivered at Place):** Seller delivers to destination
**DDP (Delivered Duty Paid):** Seller pays all duties/taxes

**Recommendation:** Use FCA or DAP for clarity

## Customs Duties and Taxes

### Duty Calculation
**Formula:** `(Product Value + Shipping + Insurance) × Duty Rate`

**Duty Rates by Region:**
- **US:** 0-37.5% (average 3.5%)
- **EU:** 0-17% (average 4%)
- **GCC:** 5% (common external tariff)
- **Nigeria:** 0-35% (average 10%)
- **South Africa:** 0-45% (average 8%)

### VAT/GST
- **EU:** 19-27% (varies by country)
- **UK:** 20%
- **UAE:** 5%
- **Saudi Arabia:** 15%
- **India:** 18%
- **Singapore:** 8%

### De Minimis Values (Duty-Free Threshold)
- **US:** $800
- **EU:** €150 (€10 for gifts)
- **UK:** £135
- **Canada:** CAD $20 (tax-free: CAD $150)
- **Australia:** AUD $1,000

## Compliance Best Practices

### For Sellers

1. **Accurate Product Classification**
   - Use correct HS codes
   - Detailed product descriptions
   - Declare all components

2. **Know Your Customer (KYC)**
   - Verify buyer identity
   - Understand end-use
   - Screen against sanctions lists

3. **Document Everything**
   - Maintain records for 7 years
   - Keep all correspondence
   - Document due diligence

4. **Stay Updated**
   - Monitor sanction list updates
   - Track regulatory changes
   - Subscribe to trade alerts

### For Buyers

1. **Understand Destination Requirements**
   - Check import restrictions
   - Verify license requirements
   - Calculate total landed cost

2. **Provide Accurate Information**
   - Correct shipping address
   - Valid import license (if required)
   - Tax/VAT registration

3. **Budget for Duties/Taxes**
   - Factor in customs duties
   - Account for VAT/GST
   - Consider brokerage fees
