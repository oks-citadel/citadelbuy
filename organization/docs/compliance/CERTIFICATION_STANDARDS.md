# Certification Standards for Broxiva Vendors

## Security & Compliance Certifications

### SOC 2 Type II
**Description:** Service Organization Control 2 attestation covering Security, Availability, Processing Integrity, Confidentiality, and Privacy.

**Recommended For:** Enterprise vendors, SaaS providers, high-transaction volume sellers

**Requirements:**
- 6-12 month observation period
- Independent CPA audit
- Documented security controls
- Annual renewal

**Benefits:**
- +30 points to trust score
- "Enterprise Verified" badge
- Preferred vendor status
- Higher transaction limits

**Cost:** $15,000 - $100,000
**Timeline:** 6-12 months

**Issuing Bodies:** AICPA-certified CPA firms (Deloitte, PwC, EY, KPMG, mid-tier firms)

---

### ISO 27001 (Information Security Management)
**Description:** International standard for Information Security Management Systems (ISMS).

**Recommended For:** All vendors processing sensitive data

**Requirements:**
- ISMS implementation (Plan-Do-Check-Act cycle)
- Risk assessment and treatment
- 114 security controls (Annex A)
- Internal audits
- Management review

**Benefits:**
- +25 points to trust score
- "Security Verified" badge
- Global recognition
- Competitive advantage

**Cost:** $10,000 - $50,000
**Validity:** 3 years (annual surveillance audits)

**Issuing Bodies:** BSI, LRQA, SGS, TÃœV, DNV

---

### ISO 9001 (Quality Management)
**Description:** Quality Management System standard.

**Recommended For:** Manufacturing vendors, product sellers

**Benefits:**
- +15 points to trust score
- "Quality Assured" badge
- Customer confidence

**Cost:** $5,000 - $30,000
**Validity:** 3 years

---

### PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard)
**Description:** Security standard for organizations handling credit card data.

**Required For:** Vendors processing card payments directly (not via Broxiva)

**Levels:**
- **Level 1:** >6M transactions/year - Full external audit
- **Level 2:** 1-6M transactions/year - Self-assessment + scan
- **Level 3:** 20K-1M transactions/year - Self-assessment + scan
- **Level 4:** <20K transactions/year - Self-assessment

**Cost:** $5,000 - $50,000 (Level 1)
**Validity:** 12 months

## Regional Certifications

### GDPR Certification (EU)
**Description:** General Data Protection Regulation compliance certification.

**Required For:** Vendors serving EU customers

**Components:**
- Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA)
- Records of Processing Activities (ROPA)
- Privacy by Design implementation
- Data Subject Rights mechanisms

**Issuing Bodies:** EU certification bodies, data protection authorities

---

### POPIA Certification (South Africa)
**Description:** Protection of Personal Information Act compliance.

**Required For:** South African vendors or those serving SA customers

**Components:**
- Information Officer designation
- Processing lawfulness assessment
- Data subject consent mechanisms
- Cross-border transfer safeguards

---

### NDPR Compliance (Nigeria)
**Description:** Nigeria Data Protection Regulation certification.

**Required For:** Nigerian vendors

**Components:**
- Data Protection Compliance Organization (DPCO) registration
- Data Protection Audit
- Privacy Policy compliant with NDPR
- Data Processing Agreement

**Issuing Body:** National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA)

---

## Industry-Specific Certifications

### Halal Certification
**Description:** Products compliant with Islamic dietary laws.

**Required For:** Food, beverages, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals sold in Muslim-majority markets

**Components:**
- Ingredient compliance (no alcohol, pork derivatives)
- Halal slaughter methods (for meat)
- Separate production lines
- Facility audit

**Validity:** 24 months

**Recognized Bodies:**
- **JAKIM** (Malaysia) - Gold standard, globally recognized
- **MUI** (Indonesia)
- **EIAC** (UAE)
- **SASO** (Saudi Arabia)
- **HFA** (Halal Food Authority - UK)

**Cost:** $500 - $5,000
**Timeline:** 2-4 months

**Benefits:**
- Access to $2 trillion Halal market
- "Halal Certified" badge
- Required for GCC markets

---

### Kosher Certification
**Description:** Products compliant with Jewish dietary laws.

**Components:**
- Ingredient verification
- Production supervision
- Equipment kashrut

**Issuing Bodies:** OU (Orthodox Union), OK Kosher, Star-K

---

## Product Safety Certifications

### CE Marking (EU)
**Description:** Product meets EU safety, health, and environmental protection standards.

**Required For:** Products sold in EU (electronics, machinery, toys, PPE, medical devices)

**Directives:**
- Low Voltage Directive (LVD)
- Electromagnetic Compatibility (EMC)
- Radio Equipment Directive (RED)
- RoHS (Restriction of Hazardous Substances)
- REACH (Chemicals regulation)

**Process:**
- Self-assessment OR Notified Body assessment
- Technical documentation
- EU Declaration of Conformity
- Affix CE mark

**Not Required:** Software, food, medical devices Class I

---

### FCC Certification (US)
**Description:** Federal Communications Commission approval for electronic devices.

**Required For:** Devices that emit radio frequency energy

**Classes:**
- **Class A:** Industrial/commercial use
- **Class B:** Residential use (stricter limits)

---

### UL Certification (US)
**Description:** Underwriters Laboratories safety certification.

**Required For:** Electrical products, building materials, safety equipment

**Benefits:** Insurance approval, market acceptance, retail requirements

---

### Energy Star (US)
**Description:** Energy efficiency certification.

**Benefits:** Government procurement, utility rebates, eco-conscious market

---

## Trade & Customs Certifications

### AEO (Authorized Economic Operator)
**Description:** Trusted trader status for customs compliance.

**Benefits:**
- Fewer customs inspections
- Priority clearance
- Reduced security requirements
- Mutual recognition agreements

**Issuing Bodies:** Customs authorities (EU, US CBP, others)

**Requirements:**
- 3+ years trading history
- Proven compliance record
- Financial solvency
- Security and safety standards

---

### C-TPAT (Customs-Trade Partnership Against Terrorism)
**Description:** US customs security program.

**Benefits:**
- Reduced inspections
- Priority processing
- Account manager

**Requirements:**
- Supply chain security assessment
- Business partner requirements
- Security policies and procedures

---

## Platform Verification Process

### Step 1: Document Submission
Upload certification documents to vendor portal:
- Certificate (PDF)
- Scope statement
- Validity dates
- Issuing body contact

### Step 2: Verification
Broxiva verifies with issuing body:
- Certificate authenticity
- Current validity
- Scope matches business

**Timeline:** 3-5 business days

### Step 3: Approval & Badge
- Badge added to vendor profile
- Trust score updated
- Listed in certified vendor directory

### Step 4: Ongoing Monitoring
- Expiry date tracking
- Renewal reminders (90, 60, 30 days)
- Annual re-verification

## Certification Roadmap by Vendor Tier

### Bronze Tier (New Vendors)
**Recommended:**
- Business registration
- Basic compliance

**Optional:**
- ISO 9001 (quality focus)

### Silver Tier (Established Vendors)
**Recommended:**
- ISO 9001 (quality)

**Optional:**
- Industry-specific (Halal, Organic, etc.)

### Gold Tier (High-Volume Vendors)
**Recommended:**
- ISO 27001 OR SOC 2 Type II
- ISO 9001
- Regional compliance certs

**Optional:**
- ISO 14001 (environmental)

### Platinum Tier (Enterprise Vendors)
**Required:**
- SOC 2 Type II OR ISO 27001
- ISO 9001

**Recommended:**
- Multiple regional certifications
- Industry leadership certifications
- B Corp or sustainability certifications
