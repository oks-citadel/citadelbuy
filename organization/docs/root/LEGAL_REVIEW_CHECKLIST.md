## Data Retention Legal Requirements

### Retention Period Definition
- [ ] Data retention schedule established for each data category
- [ ] **Customer Account Data** - retention period defined (e.g., duration of account + 7 years)
- [ ] **Transaction Records** - minimum 7 years for tax purposes
- [ ] **Payment Card Data** - no storage of CVV; limited PAN storage
- [ ] **Marketing Communications** - retention until consent withdrawal
- [ ] **Customer Support Records** - 3-7 years typical
- [ ] **Website Analytics** - 26-50 months typical (GDPR consideration)
- [ ] **Legal and Compliance Records** - statute of limitations + reasonable period
- [ ] **Employee Records** - jurisdiction-specific requirements
- [ ] **Contract Archives** - duration of contract + 7 years

### Legal and Regulatory Requirements
- [ ] Tax record retention requirements met (typically 7 years)
- [ ] SEC/financial regulations retention requirements (if applicable)
- [ ] SOX compliance retention (if applicable)
- [ ] HIPAA retention requirements (if handling health data)
- [ ] Employment law retention requirements
- [ ] Product liability statute of limitations considered
- [ ] Contract statute of limitations considered
- [ ] Warranty period + reasonable time retention
- [ ] Legal hold process for litigation documented
- [ ] E-discovery readiness assessed

### Data Minimization and Deletion
- [ ] Automated deletion processes implemented
- [ ] Manual review triggers for retention exceptions
- [ ] Anonymization as alternative to deletion considered
- [ ] Pseudonymization implemented where appropriate
- [ ] Deletion verification and certification process
- [ ] Backup tape/archive deletion procedures
- [ ] Cloud storage deletion verification
- [ ] Third-party processor deletion confirmed
- [ ] Hard copy/physical record destruction procedures
- [ ] Secure deletion methods employed (overwrites, degaussing)

### Exceptions to Deletion
- [ ] Legal hold process suspends normal deletion

- [ ] Regulatory investigation preservation

- [ ] Legitimate interest override documented
- [ ] Public interest archiving (rare)
- [ ] Statistical purposes with anonymization

### Privacy Law Requirements
- [ ] GDPR storage limitation principle compliance

- [ ] State-specific data retention laws reviewed
- [ ] Right to erasure honored unless exception applies
- [ ] Purpose limitation enforced (no retention beyond original purpose)

### Backup and Archive Management
