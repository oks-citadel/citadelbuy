**Incident Severity Levels:**
- **Critical:** Regulatory enforcement action, major data breach, significant legal exposure
- **High:** Potential regulatory action, reportable breach, significant user impact
- **Medium:** Compliance gap with moderate impact, remediable without major consequences
- **Low:** Minor deviation, no regulatory or user impact, easily correctable

**Incident Response Process:**
1. Detection and reporting
2. Initial assessment and severity classification
3. Incident response team activation
4. Investigation and root cause analysis
5. Remediation and corrective actions
6. Documentation and reporting (internal and external as required)
7. Lessons learned and process improvement

**Reportable Incidents (to regulators):**
- Data breaches affecting personal information (GDPR: 72 hours; CCPA: as required by state laws)
- Payment card data breaches (PCI DSS: immediately to payment brands and acquirer)
- Significant consumer harm or deceptive practices (FTC: as required)
